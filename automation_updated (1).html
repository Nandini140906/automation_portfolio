After every meeting, tasks got lost.

Someone had to write up decisions, assign tasks, and send it all out. Things got missed. Teams lost momentum.

How It Works

2

3

Full meeting record stored — organized and searchable.
4

Each person gets their tasks and deadlines. No noise.
The Solution
One form. Every task distributed. Zero manual work.

Submit the transcript and walk away. Tasks are in inboxes before the team opens their laptops.

No follow-up emails. No confusion. No momentum lost after the call ends.

AI Lead Gen & Automated Follow-Up

Turning missed leads into managed opportunities automatically.

n8nTallyGoogle SheetsGemini AIGmail
The Problem
Leads were slipping through the cracks.

Forms sat unanswered. Agents followed up hours too late. The lead had already moved on.

How It Works
1
Lead fills the Tally form
Requirements and contact details submitted.
2
Data saves to Google Sheets
Captured and organized instantly — no manual entry.
3
Gemini AI qualifies the lead
Summarizes what the lead is looking for and key signals.
4
Two emails sent automatically
Lead gets acknowledged. Agency gets the qualified summary — instantly.
The Solution
Every lead gets a response in seconds — with full context.

Form submitted → data logged → lead acknowledged → agency gets a qualified summary. No delay. No manual work.

Business Impact
Faster response. Better conversion.

Agents start the day with qualified leads already in their inbox. Less chasing. More closing.
